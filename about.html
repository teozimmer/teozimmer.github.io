Bio

Teo Zimmer is a Composer, Arranger and Sound designer from Sweden. He specializes in video games, but also writes for live concerts and orchestras. If the occasion he's writing for is suitable, he loves to experiment with polyrythms, polymeters, uneven subdivisions and sometimes microtonality, especially in ways that are a bit more subtle and achieves an effect that is enjoyable to listeners who do not know of these concepts as well.
